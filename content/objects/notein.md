### [notein]

INLETS:

- NONE.

OUTLETS:

- n: (number depends on number of arguments)

- 1st: 
 
  - float - MIDI note number.
  
- 2nd: 

  - float - MIDI velocity
  
- 3rd: 

  - float - MIDI channel/port
  
ARGUMENTS:

- float - MIDI channel/port

> see also [[ctlin]](../ctlin) (etc) - MIDI in objects.

> see also [[noteout]](../noteout) (etc) - MIDI out objects.

> updated for Pd version 0.48-2
