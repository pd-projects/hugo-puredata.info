### [polytouchout]

INLETS:

- 1st: 
 
  - float - MIDI aftertouch value.
  
- 2nd: 

  - float - MIDI note number
  
- 3rd: 

  - float - channel/port

OUTLETS:
  
- NONE.
  
ARGUMENTS:

- float - channel/port


> see also [[notein]](../notein) (etc) - MIDI in objects.

> see also [[noteout]](../noteout) (etc) - MIDI out objects.

> updated for Pd version 0.48-2
