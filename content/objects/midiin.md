### [midiin]

INLETS:

- NONE.

OUTLETS:

- 1st: 
 
  - float - raw MIDI byte by byte (except real-time messages)
  
- 2nd: 

  - float - MIDI channel/port
  
ARGUMENTS:

- NONE.

> see also [[noteout]](../noteout) (etc) - MIDI out objects.
